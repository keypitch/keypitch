How to Change the Pitch of a YouTube Song in Real Time

To change the pitch of any YouTube song in real time directly in the browser — no downloads, no converters.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

download

Download the KeyPitch Chrome extension using the button below.

open_in_new Download on Chrome Web Store
music_note

Open YouTube and play the song of choice.

extension

Click the puzzle icon (Extensions) at the top-right of Chrome, then select KeyPitch.

tune

When the control panel opens, move the pitch slider left to lower the pitch (down to 415.3 Hz) or right to raise it (up to 466.2 Hz).

star_rate

If the experience is helpful, consider leaving a 5/5 rating on the Chrome Web Store.

More Ways to Control Music

The tool can also transpose by semitones to change the key in real time — ideal for singers and instrumental practice.

It supports reverb, speed adjustment, loop creation, and an 8D audio mode for immersive listening.
